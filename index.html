Speed Cuber

Learn more about my speedcubing journey and achievements here

Ellis Brocklesby is an avid speedcuber with a diverse skill set in solving various puzzles. He averages 22 seconds in 3x3 speed cubing, with a personal
best of 9 seconds, and consistently achieves an impressive 5-second average in 2x2.
His passion for cubing extends beyond these two, as he enjoys tackling a wide range of puzzles, including 4x4, Pyraminx,
Skewb, OH 3x3, 5x5, and more. Ellis's dedication to the craft showcases his commitment to mastering the art of speedcubing.
